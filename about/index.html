About Me

2020-04-25

Since you’re here, this is the time for me to introduce myself.

My name is Anish Bhobe and as you can guess from my projects, I’m a huge enthusiast of computer graphics and C++ in game development.

I at the moment of writing I am a fortnight (not the game) away from finishing my last academic requirement to get the paper that declares that I may have learnt a few things at Birla Institute of Science and Technology, Pilani - K. K. Birla Goa Campus. (Yes, quite a long name)

When my day’s classes is over - you’ll find me postured in front of elven c++ scripts and tabs full of articles on how to implement the techniques I feel like adding.
Needless to say, I love videogames - the immersive worlds that are realistic, artistic or both (which I absolutely want to be responsible in bringing to life) and the amazing narratives and stories that they deliver.

Now that I am finally getting out of this college - I believe it’s time that I actually showcase my things.
